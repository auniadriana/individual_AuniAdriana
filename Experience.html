Experiences in Life




My first experience of working was when I was 18. I was waiting for my SPM result and decided to try my luck finding a job that accept a part-timer. I got lucky when my friend told me there was a vacancy at a clothes shop in GM Klang. I went there and successfully become a part-timer for the first time in my life. It was exhilirating and totally a good experience. It was good to know the great feeling of having a job. There were some ups and downs but everything was worth a try. I only work there for 1 month as I have to attend an important business. Nonetheless, I got along with my colleague and know a thing or two about business.
